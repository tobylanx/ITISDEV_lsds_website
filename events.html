National Asian High School Debate Championship

Event Link

The National Asians High School Debate Championship (NASHDC) is the largest high school debate tournament in the country. La Salle Debate Society hosts the championship annually and invites debaters from several regions and cities to participate. Participants of NASHDC get the opportunity to engage in themes that spotlight several social issues, such as national politics, media, and gender, and the finer details surrounding the implications of these topics.
