What is LSDS?

The La Salle Debate Society (LSDS) belongs to the academic and extracurricular activities industry, specifically focusing on competitive debate, intellectual discourse, and public speaking. It fosters critical thinking, argumentation, and communication skills among students while actively engaging in national and international debate tournaments.

Its History

Founded in 1922 as the La Salle Forensics Team, LSDS has evolved into one of the most respected debating institutions in the Philippines and Asia. The organization has undergone multiple name changes, transitioning from Forensics to Forensic Debate before becoming the La Salle Debate Society. Over the years, LSDS has consistently participated in and hosted prestigious debate competitions, bringing recognition to De La Salle University (DLSU) through numerous national and international achievements.

Vision

Mission

  • Introduce the sport of debate to Lasallians with honor and integrity.
  • Develop debaters and judges through extensive training and mentorship from experienced coaches and alumni.
  • Promote sportsmanship and humility in competitions.
  • Organize events that contribute to the broader Philippine debate community.
